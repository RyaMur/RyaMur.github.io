The Bored Gamer

— Ryan Murphy

For many years I spent my recreational time immersed in the world of videogaming. The characters that I could be, the places I could go and the things that I could do fascinated me. While I still have fond memories of those times, the fact remains that they were solitary adventures and lacked true social interaction.

My desire for something allowing more interaction with friends and family led me to a new interest, the boardgaming hobby. While many of the board games that I enjoy still have the elements of interesting characters, fantastic places and exciting quests; the board game format allows me to share these experiences with others making them feel somehow more genuine and lasting.

I have created this site as an outlet for sharing my thoughts and experiences with boardgaming. If you would like to receive information and updates feel free to sign up, and if you just want to drop me a line please do. And as always,
Happy Gaming!
