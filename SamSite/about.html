Samantha was born on the Oregon Coast and has been drawing, coloring, and painting since she first learned to hold a pencil. At an early age she had dreams of becoming an animator for Disney, and on top of her homework sheâ€™d always have a requested Disney character or two to draw for her friends. As she grew in her abilities, she was attracted in the direction of classic realism, and won several awards throughout her schooling for it. Aside from her college art experience, Samantha attributes most of her abilities to her high school art teacher, and to God for giving her the talent. Along with tending her growing family, Samantha enjoys creating pieces of art in her spare time, most of which are by commission. Pencil drawing is her favorite medium, but she also enjoys acrylic painting, watercolor, and pastels. She hopes to one day find her niche in the art world as a full time artist.
